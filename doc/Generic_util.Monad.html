IO Monad



Similar to the Haskell IO Monad. The values are computations that may carry effects. The monadic bind is used to sequence the effects.

The constructor Generic_util_monad.IO and synonym Generic_util_monad.embed_io allow to embed an effectful computation in the monad. Generic_util_monad.run_io runs it.
